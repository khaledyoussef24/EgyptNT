About The Egyptian Football Associatio

The Egyptian Football Association is the governing body of football in Egypt. A member of FIFA since 1923 and a founding member of CAF, the EFA has jurisdiction for the Egyptian football league system and is in charge of the men's and women's national teams. The EFA headquarters is located in Gezira, Cairo.

EFA PRISDENT

Ahmed Megahed

M. Ahmed Mujahid was born in Al-Hamoul in Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate. He was born on March 27, 1972. He obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Department of Chemistry. He is considered one of those who devoted his entire life to football, and he progressed through his career in football, starting from Al-Hamoul to Al-Jabalayya, as follows: - Member of the Board of Directors of Al-Hamoul Club. Then he was president of the club from 1995 to 2011. Member of the Board of Directors of the Egyptian Football Association for the period from 2012-2016. Member of the Board of Directors of the Egyptian Football Association.
